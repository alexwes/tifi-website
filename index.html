About

Estefania Bustamante, the owner and principal of Art & Design Consulting LLC, a boutique consultancy firm located in New York City, specializes in meticulously crafted interiors tailored to the unique preferences and requirements of her clients. With a profound background in the fine art industry, Estefania adeptly guides clients through art acquisitions, whether they are budding enthusiasts or seasoned collectors with discerning tastes. Her firm is also renowned for its interior design services, which are characterized by contemporary elegance, the incorporation of timeless elements, and classic aesthetics. In the art selection process, Estefania dedicates meticulous attention to each client's specific objectives and desires. Central to her design philosophy is not only comprehending the client's vision but also capturing their essence, ensuring the functionality of the space for their daily needs. Art & Design Consulting LLC operates in residential and commercial projects across the United States, Argentina, and Uruguay.
